What is the difference between a civil case and a family case?

A civil suit (governed by the CPC) involves breaches of contract, property damages, and financial injunctions heard in civil judicature. A family case operates under the specialized Family Courts Act, 1964, utilizing distinct, expedited procedures strictly for marriage dissolution, maintenance, dower recovery, and child custody.

Can I get a stay order immediately?

Securing an ad-interim injunction (stay order) requires demonstrating a "prima facie" case, balance of convenience, and irreparable loss. If these rigorous legal criteria are met in your document suite, MR Law Associates frequently secures stay orders against illegal demolitions or property alienations within 48 to 72 hours.

Do you assist with NADRA document errors?

We are the premier consultancy in Karachi for resolving municipal impossibilities. When NADRA unequivocally refuses to correct a Date of Birth or parentage, we initiate the required legal declaratory suits to legally compel the amendment of the permanent national database.

How are legal consultation fees processed?

Every case is uniquely complex. We conduct an initial consultation (which may carry a fee depending on the volume of documents requiring review). If you subsequently formally execute a retainer agreement and Vakalatnama with the firm, the initial consultation outlay is conventionally credited toward the overall case fee.
