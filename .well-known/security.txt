# SECURITY POLICY SUMMARY
# =============================================================================

We take the security of our website and client data seriously.

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to us immediately
at info@mrlawassociates.com. We appreciate responsible disclosure and will
work with you to resolve any issues promptly.

Please DO NOT:
- Access or modify data without authorization
- Disrupt or degrade our services
- Use automated tools that could cause harm

We DO appreciate:
- Reports of potential security vulnerabilities
- Suggestions for improving our security posture
- Responsible disclosure practices

We will:
- Acknowledge receipt of your report within 48 hours
- Investigate and validate reported issues promptly
- Work to remediate confirmed vulnerabilities
- Recognize researchers who report valid issues (with permission)

Thank you for helping keep MR Law Associates and our clients secure.
