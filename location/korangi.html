Lawyer in Korangi Karachi

Korangi is one of Karachi's largest industrial zones, home to thousands of factories, textile mills, and manufacturing units. This sprawling industrial area, along with its residential sectors (Sector 33 through Sector 52), forms the economic backbone of Karachi's manufacturing sector. The area's large working-class population faces unique legal needs including worker compensation claims, industrial accident cases, and labor rights protection.
