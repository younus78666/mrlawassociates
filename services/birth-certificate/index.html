Birth Certificate Services

Hassle-free birth certificate registration, issuance, and correction through the Union Council.

A birth certificate is an essential document for school admission, CNIC issuance, passport applications, and various legal matters. MR Law Associates assists in obtaining new birth certificates, duplicate copies, and name/date corrections from the Union Council in Karachi.

Our Services

New Registration

First-time birth registration with the Union Council for newborns and late registrations.

Duplicate Copy

Obtaining duplicate birth certificates when originals are lost, damaged, or misplaced.

Corrections

Name, date, or parentage corrections on existing birth certificates through proper legal channels.

NADRA Linkage

Ensuring NADRA records align with Union Council birth certificates for smooth CNIC processing.
