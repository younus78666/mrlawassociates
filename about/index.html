About Reema Jawed

Advocate High Court & Lead Consultant at MR Law Associates, Karachi.

Our Mission

MR Law Associates was founded with a clear mission: to provide accessible, professional, and confidential legal assistance to the people of Karachi. Under the leadership of Advocate Reema Jawed, our consultancy bridges the gap between complex legal procedures and the everyday citizen.

With a specialization in Family Law, Civil Litigation, Criminal Defense, and Business Compliance, Reema Jawed has built a reputation for meticulous preparation, strong courtroom advocacy, and genuine client care. Her practice extends beyond legal representation to include documentation services such as Union Council certificates, educational document recovery, and FBR tax filings.
