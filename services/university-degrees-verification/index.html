The Three-Stage Higher Education Attestation Chain

Authenticating a university degree (BA, BSc, BBA, MA, MSc, PhD) dictates navigating a firm sequential pathway. HEC absolutely refuses to attest higher credentials without proof of foundational education verification.

1. University / Controller Verification

The foundational step. For certain foreign evaluators (like WES) or specific HEC protocols, the originating university (e.g., University of Karachi, NED, IBA, SZABIST) must visually inspect their internal ledgers to confirm the graduate's transcript integrity. This typically concludes with securely sealing the verified documents in an official registrar envelope.

2. HEC Security Attestation

The Higher Education Commission stands as the sole federal body authorized to authenticate university degrees. HEC cross-references the university charter and applies a unique, traceable security ticket and embossment directly onto the original degree and transcript. Crucial Note: HEC mandates you submit your IBCC-attested Matric and Inter certificates alongside your degree application.

3. MOFA & Foreign Consulate Approval

Finally, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MOFA) apostilles or attests the HEC officer's signature. Following MOFA verification, the degree becomes eligible for presentation to Middle Eastern or European consular offices located within Pakistan for final visa stamping.

Frequently Asked Questions About Degree Verification

What is HEC degree attestation in Pakistan?

The Higher Education Commission places a specialized security stamp and signature on original degrees, serving as the definitive federal proof that the issuing university was officially chartered and the academic award is authentic.

Do I need my matric and inter certificates for HEC?

Yes, absolutely. HEC strictly commands the presentation of your foundational secondary certificates—which must already bear the IBCC verification stamps—before they will touch your university degree.

What is a sealed transcript for WES evaluation?

Foreign evaluators demand your Karachi university places official transcripts into institutional envelopes, sealing them with a cross-flap signature and stamp to guarantee against transit tampering or forgery.

Can I get my degree attested while abroad?

Yes. Send your original portfolio to MR Law Associates via international courier. We execute the complete verification sequences as your authorized legal proxy in Karachi.

How long does degree verification take?

Initial university verifications mandate 7 to 21 days depending on internal registrar backlogs. Subsequent HEC processing and MOFA apostilles add roughly an additional 2 to 3 weeks.

Why would HEC reject my degree?

Prime rejection causes include: laminated original documents, significant discrepancies in identity spellings across the academic chain, or missing previous attestation stamps from IBCC.
