Succession Case

Expert legal assistance for obtaining succession certificates and resolving inheritance matters.

After the passing of a family member, legal heirs often need a succession certificate to access bank accounts, transfer properties, and claim assets. MR Law Associates provides end-to-end assistance in obtaining succession certificates from the Civil Court.

Probate of Will

Assistance in the legal probate process for testamentary wills and their enforcement through court.

Obtaining Letters of Administration when no will exists, establishing the legal right to administer the estate.
