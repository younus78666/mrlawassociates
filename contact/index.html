How to Prepare for Your Legal Consultation

To extract maximal strategic value from your initial legal consultation in Karachi, we strongly advise gathering all pertinent documentation prior to your appointment.

Property & Civil Disputes

Bring all original (or clear copies) of title deeds, lease agreements, sale agreements, previous court stay orders, and threatening legal notices you have received.

Family & Divorce Cases

Ensure you have the registered Nikah Nama, computerized marriage certificate, child birth certificates, and detailed lists of the dowry articles in dispute.
