Death Certificate Services

Official death certificate registration and issuance through the Union Council.

A death certificate is a critical legal document required for inheritance proceedings, property transfer, pension claims, and insurance. MR Law Associates provides complete assistance in obtaining death certificates from the Union Council in Karachi.

Our Assistance

New Registration

Registration of death with the Union Council including proper documentation and hospital/graveyard records.

Late Registration

Assistance with late death registration when the death was not reported within the statutory period.

Duplicate Copy

Obtaining duplicate death certificates for lost or damaged originals from the Union Council records.

NADRA Update

Updating NADRA records to reflect the deceased's status for family CNIC and inheritance purposes.
