Marriage Certificate Services

Official marriage certificate registration and Nikah Nama attestation through the Union Council.

A valid marriage certificate (Nikah Nama) registered with the Union Council is a legal prerequisite for many official procedures in Pakistan, including visa applications, passport changes, and property transfers. MR Law Associates ensures your marriage is properly documented.

What We Handle

Nikah Registration

Assistance with proper Nikah Nama registration through authorized Nikah Registrars and Union Council.

Court Marriage

Legal facilitation of court marriages with proper documentation, witnesses, and judicial validation.

Attestation

Marriage certificate attestation from the Union Council, District Council, and Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

Duplicate Copy

Obtaining duplicate marriage certificates from the Union Council for lost or damaged originals.
